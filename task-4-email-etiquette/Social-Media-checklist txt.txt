Social Media DO's

1. Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for all platforms to ensure you do not allow unauthorized users to gain access.

2. Utilize the "Privacy Settings" to control who can view your personal contact information and location.

3. Before you post anything, remember that your "Digital Footprint" is permanent and viewable by potential recruiters.

4. Make sure you have strong and unique passwords for all of your social media accounts.

5. Respect people's privacy by asking permission before posting pictures of them.

Social Media DON'Ts

1. Avoid posting pictures of your ID cards, admit cards, or boarding passes, as information leaks through QR codes.

2. Avoid clicking on "Free Giveaway" or "Survey" messages from unknown accounts, as it may be a Phishing threat.

3. Avoid accepting friend/follow requests from accounts that seem suspicious or have no mutual friends.

4. Avoid tagging your "Live Location" on your post, as you should be out of the location for your physical safety.

5. Avoid cyber-bullying or using hate speech, as it may lead to legal actions against you through IT laws.
